Cutting Diet Plan - Scrollable Reel

6:30–7:00 AM - Early Morning

  • 1 glass warm water with lemon
  • 4–5 soaked almonds + 2 walnuts
  • Optional: Black coffee or green tea (no sugar)

8:30–9:00 AM - Breakfast

  • Option 1: 3 boiled eggs (2 whole, 1 white) + 2 multigrain toasts + 1 apple
  • Option 2: 1 bowl oats with milk + 1 tsp peanut butter + 1 boiled egg

11:00–11:30 AM - Mid-Morning Snack

  • 1 fruit (e.g. papaya, orange, guava)
  • OR 100g boiled chickpeas or sprouts with lemon and salt

1:00–2:00 PM - Lunch

  • Option 1: 1 cup brown rice or 2 multigrain rotis + 1 cup grilled chicken + salad
  • Option 2: 1 bowl dal + 1 bowl vegetables + 1 roti + salad + low-fat curd

5:00–6:00 PM - Evening Snack

  • Option 1: 1 boiled egg + black coffee
  • Option 2: 1 fruit + roasted chana
  • Option 3: Homemade moong chilla

7:30–8:30 PM - Dinner

  • Option 1: 150g grilled fish or chicken + boiled/stir-fried veggies + salad
  • Option 2: 100g paneer/tofu + vegetable soup + salad

Optional (Late Night)

  • Herbal tea or chamomile tea
  • OR 1 glass lukewarm turmeric milk (low fat)

Tips for Cutting

  • Drink 3–4 liters of water daily
  • Sleep 7–8 hours for better recovery
  • One cheat meal per week is allowed

Daily Supplement (Optional)

  • Whey Protein (Post-Workout)
  • Omega-3 Capsules
  • Multivitamins
  • Vitamin D (if deficient)
  • Creatine (3-5g/day, optional)
